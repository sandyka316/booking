About BookSpace

Your go-to platform for finding and booking the perfect co-working space.

Our Mission

At BookSpace, we believe that the environment you work in greatly impacts your productivity and creativity. Our mission is to connect professionals with inspiring workspaces that meet their unique needs. Whether you're a freelancer looking for a quiet space to focus, a team in need of a collaborative meeting room, or a startup searching for your next headquarters, BookSpace has got you covered.

How We Started

BookSpace was founded in 2023 by a group of remote workers who struggled to find suitable places to work from. After bouncing between coffee shops, libraries, and makeshift home offices, they realized there was a need for a platform that would streamline the process of finding and booking professional workspaces.

What started as a simple idea has grown into a comprehensive booking platform with hundreds of spaces across multiple cities.
